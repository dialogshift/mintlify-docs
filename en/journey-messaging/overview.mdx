Dialogshift Journey Messaging enables you to reach your guests through various channels. Based on events, you can set up flows that provide your guests with information.

## What is Journey Messaging?

Journey Messaging is a powerful tool that allows you to create automated communication flows for your guests. You can:

- **Multi-channel communication** coordinated across different platforms
- **Event-based messages** that respond to specific guest actions
- **Personalized content** based on guest data and behavior
- **Time-controlled flows** that activate at the optimal moment

## Use Cases

With Journey Messaging you can cover various scenarios:

- **Welcome messages** after booking
- **Check-in reminders** before arrival
- **Service information** during the stay
- **Feedback requests** after check-out
- **Special offers** for returning guests
