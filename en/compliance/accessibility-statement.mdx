# Accessibility Statement for Your DialogShift Chat Widget

## Important Note on the Accessibility Strengthening Act

<Warning>
From **June 28, 2025**, accessibility legislation comes into effect in various regions. As a hotel operator or website operator using our DialogShift chat widget, you might choose to publish an accessibility statement on your website.
</Warning>

## How could you proceed?

### 1. Download accessibility statement

Download our accessibility statement for the DialogShift chat widget:

<Card title="Accessibility Statement PDF" icon="file-pdf" href="https://assets.dialogshift.com/documentation/ds-barrierefreiheitsstaerkungsgesetz.pdf">
  Download accessibility statement
</Card>

**URL:** https://assets.dialogshift.com/documentation/ds-barrierefreiheitsstaerkungsgesetz.pdf

### 2. Integration into your existing accessibility statement

You have **two options** to integrate our widget statement:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Option A: Embedding">
    - Add the content of our PDF to your existing accessibility statement
    - Add a section "External Widgets" or "DialogShift Chat Widget"
    - Your visitors find all information in one place
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Option B: Linking">
    - Mention the DialogShift chat widget in your existing accessibility statement
    - Link from there to our PDF for download
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
