<Note>
  To create a user account at DialogShift, you must have been invited by an admin of your organization in the DialogShift app. New users can be invited [here](https://app.dialogshift.com/user-management).
</Note>

## Step-by-Step Process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You receive an invitation to DialogShift via email">
    Follow the link in the email to the registration page of the DialogShift App
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign username and password">
    Now assign a password and a username that will appear in the chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter code from email">
    Our system has sent you an email with a six-digit code to verify your email address. Enter it now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to DialogShift">
    Almost done! You can now sign in to the DialogShift App with your email address and assigned password.
  </Step>
</Steps>
