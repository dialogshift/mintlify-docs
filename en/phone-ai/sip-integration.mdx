## What we need from you

<Note>
  Your phone system must be SIP-capable and allow registrations via the internet.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up SIP account">
    Separate account specifically for Phone AI
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide access data">
    Complete SIP access data (see below)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Internal speed dials">
    At least one number is required. Transfers to additional departments or teams are optional.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required SIP Access Data

**Always required:**

- Username (without spaces)
- Password
- Endpoint (URL or IP address)
- Port

**If available:**

- Proxy (URL or IP address)

## Additional Requirements

### Local Phone Systems

You need **one** of the following options:

- **Fixed IP address** (IPv4 or IPv6)
- **Dynamic DNS** (due to possible interruptions during IP changes)

<Info>
  Cloud phone systems are already accessible via the internet.
</Info>

### Internal Transfers

Please provide us with:

- Speed dials for departments
- Special extensions (emergencies, VIP)
- Time-based transfer rules
